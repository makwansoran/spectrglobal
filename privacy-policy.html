Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Who We Are

SPECTR AS provides an online catalog for vehicle parts, customer support, order handling, and related services. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information when you use our website or contact us.

Information We Collect

We may collect account details, contact information, delivery details, order history, cart activity, vehicle fitment selections, VIN or registration inputs that you choose to provide, support messages, and technical information such as browser type, device data, IP address, cookies, and analytics events.

How We Use Information

  • To show compatible parts and improve product recommendations.
  • To process orders, payments, delivery, returns, and customer service requests.
  • To secure the website, prevent fraud, and maintain reliable operations.
  • To measure website performance and improve the shopping experience.
  • To send transactional messages and, where permitted, marketing updates.

Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, maintain shopping functionality, understand traffic, and improve the website. You can control cookies in your browser settings. Some features may not work correctly if necessary cookies are disabled.

Payments and Partners

Payments may be handled by trusted payment providers such as Stripe or other checkout partners. We do not store full payment card details on our own servers. Delivery, analytics, hosting, authentication, and customer support providers may process information only as needed to provide their services.

Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described here, including order records, legal obligations, accounting requirements, security, and dispute resolution.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection to certain processing of your personal information. You may also withdraw consent where processing depends on consent.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information. No online service is completely risk-free, but we work to keep customer information secure and limit access to those who need it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when our services, legal requirements, or data practices change. The latest version will always be published on this page.

Questions about privacy? Contact SPECTR AS through support.
