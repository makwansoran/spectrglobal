About

Spectr wraps the open-source AI Hedge Fund stack: FastAPI on port 8000, multi-agent LangGraph orchestration, and your choice of LLM provider. This page is the public shell; the engine runs locally or on your infrastructure.

  • Backend — POST /hedge-fund/run (SSE)
  • Agents — GET /hedge-fund/agents
  • Health — GET /ping
  • Stack — Python · FastAPI · LangGraph
  • Disclaimer — Educational use only; not investment advice.
